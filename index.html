Student Feedback System

Final year project | Jan, 2017 - May, 2017

Its an online feedback system that takes feedback from students about the teaching styles of the instructors, subject coverage, workload of the subject etc. through questionaire in an easy and quick manner and enhances the overall experience of taking feedback compared to the earlier complex manual process based on pen and paper.

After taking feedback from students the system generates comprehensive report under defferent categories. The report can be visualized in the form of pie-chart and can also be exported to Excel/PDF format.

The overall goal of the project is to enhance the teaching style and with that make learning more effective and relevant.
